Full guide: https://github.com/MLopez-Ibanez/irace

Install:
1) Install R.
2) R -e "install.packages('irace')"

Running irace:
1) cd to the tuning folder.
2) R --silent -e "library(irace);system.file(package='irace')"
3) Copy the folder that gets printed.
4) *folder*/bin/irace --parallel x, where x is the amount of threads you want.
